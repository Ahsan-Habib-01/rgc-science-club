Our Activities

Innovation • Research • Excellence

At RGC Science Club, we believe that science is not just about memorizing facts, but about exploring the unknown. From theoretical discussions to practical applications, our club serves as a vibrant ecosystem for young minds. We bridge the gap between academic curriculum and real-world innovation through a structured series of activities designed to challenge and inspire.

"Somantoral" Magazine

Our flagship publication, "Somantoral" (Parallel), is the heartbeat of our intellectual community. It is more than just a magazine; it is a platform for student voices. Published monthly, it features peer-reviewed research articles, science fiction stories, mind-bending mathematical puzzles, and updates on the latest global scientific breakthroughs.

Written, edited, and designed entirely by our members, Somantoral represents the creative and analytical potential of Rangpur Govt. College students.

Research & Development

The Research Wing is the core of our club's mission. We encourage students to dive deep into scientific inquiry. Our research activities are structured into three phases: Hypothesis, Experimentation, and Conclusion.

Currently, our teams are working on projects related to Applied Physics, Environmental Biology, and Data Science. We guide students on how to write standard research papers, conduct literature reviews, and analyze data effectively, preparing them for higher academic pursuits.

Workshops & Seminars

Learning never stops at RGC Science Club. We organize weekly workshops focused on skill development that goes beyond the classroom.

  • Programming & CS: Hands-on coding sessions in C, C++, and Python.
  • Robotics: Introduction to Arduino, circuit design, and automation.
  • Astronomy: Stargazing nights and seminars on Astrophysics.

We also host monthly seminars featuring guest professors and industry experts who share insights on cutting-edge topics like Quantum Mechanics and AI.

Science Fairs & Projects

Innovation needs a stage. Every year, we organize the RGC Science Carnival, where students showcase their projects to a wider audience. From renewable energy models to AI-based software solutions, our members turn ideas into prototypes.

These events not only foster creativity but also teach critical skills such as public speaking, project management, and teamwork.
